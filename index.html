Underlined text needs a phone call or a second look before relying on it. Nursery lines always tally at $0. When you check more than one source for the same species, the sheet automatically fills the need cheapest-first — free stock, then the lowest price, working up — no matter what order you tap the boxes in. Each source only gets charged for whatever's still left after the cheaper ones are used, so checking an extra, pricier box after the need's already covered always shows $0 for it. A note appears under each species name once you've checked anything for it: "still short N" means keep sourcing; "covered" means stop, nothing more is needed there.
