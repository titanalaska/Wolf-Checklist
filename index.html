Greyed-out lines are dead ends — nothing available there, nothing to check. Everything else, including our own nursery stock, is an open checkbox — nothing is pre-approved. Underlined notes need a phone call or a look at the tag before relying on them. Nursery lines always tally at $0 — it's stock we already own, not a new cost. Checking boxes for a species fills the "need" number in the order you check them — the first box absorbs cost up to its own quantity toward that species's need, and each source after that only costs for whatever's still left, not its full lot. A small note appears under each species name once you've checked anything for it: "still short N" means keep checking more sources for that one; "covered" means you're done there — checking further boxes for it will show $0 since nothing more is needed. Uncheck and recheck in a different order if you want to change which source gets used first. Lines with a price but no stated quantity (assumed to cover whatever's left) and lines with no price yet both show as "+N pending" per source in the tally.
